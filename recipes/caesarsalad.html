Caesar Salad Recipe

Caesar Salad is a green salad of romaine lettuce and croutons dressed lemon juice, olive oil and Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients:

  • Romaine or Lettuce
  • Crushed Garlic
  • Dressing(Italian or any dressing desired)
  • Croutons
  • Lemon Juice
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Steps:

  • 1. In a small bowl, whisk the ingredients. Place romaine/lettuce in a large bowl.
  • 2. Drizzle with dressing and toss to coat.
  • 3. Serve with salad croutons and additional cheese if desired.
