Chicken Adobo Recipe

Chicken Adobo is the national dish of the Philippines that is both sweet and savory with a distinct soy and a hint of vinegar flavor .

Ingredients:

  • Chicken thighs and/or drumsticks
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Whole Pepper Corn
  • Bay Leaves

Steps:

  • 1. In a shallow dish, combine the first 5 ingredients. Add chicken; refrigerate, covered, 20-30 minutes. Drain, reserving marinade. Pat chicken dry.
  • 2. In a large skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat; brown chicken. Stir in water and reserved marinade. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered, until chicken is no longer pink and sauce is slightly reduced, 20-25 minutes.
  • 3. Discard bay leaf. If desired, serve chicken with cooking sauce.
